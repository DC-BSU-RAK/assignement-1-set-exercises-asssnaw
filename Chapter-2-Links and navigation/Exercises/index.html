Overview

""Stranger Things" is a thrilling Netflix original that combines science fiction, horror, and 80s nostalgia. The story unfolds in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, where a boy disappears under mysterious circumstances. His friends, a girl with psychokinetic powers, and a single mother who is determined to find him uncover a terrifying alternate dimension known as the Upside Down.

Key Information

• Genre: Sci-fi, Horror and Drama
• Network: Netflix
