🍗 BANG BANG CHICKEN BITES 🍗

Bang Bang Chicken Bites are crispy, spicy, and full of flavor!
These little nuggets are baked or air-fried, then tossed in a creamy chili sauce.
Perfect for parties, snacks, or any time you crave something with a kick!

INGREDIENTS

  • 24 ounces frozen crispy chicken nuggets
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha hot sauce (more if desired)
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions

METHOD

  1. Preheat air fryer to 300°F (149°C).
  2. Cook chicken nuggets for 10 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through.
  3. Or bake in oven at 400°F (204°C) for 12 minutes.
  4. Mix mayo, sweet chili sauce, and Sriracha in a large bowl.
  5. Toss hot nuggets in sauce until evenly coated.
  6. Top with green onions and serve immediately.

🔥 Enjoy your crispy, creamy Bang Bang Chicken Bites! 🔥
